Why is there a charge on my card?

Who is 7CBilled.us? 7CBilled.us is a billing entry for Seventh Compass, Inc. Seventh Compass, Inc. is a software development company in Gainesville, Florida, that makes software used by churches, non-profits, and small businesses. We have been in business since 2003.

Why are you charging us? This is either a recurring charge for txtsignal.com, Simply Youth Ministry's Tools software suite (including SimplyTXT, Communicate, Team, or SYM Jobs Board); or, for a one-time charge for LIVE Curriculum.

Doesn't sound familiar? If any of the above products or services doesn't sound familiar, please ask around your office. It's likely that someone in your organization has signed up for one of these services.

Can we get an invoice for the charges? Yes. Actually, we will have already sent one Every time we send a charge through we send a receipt to the email to the person (you or your co-worker) that signed up for the account. If the charge is for txtsignal.com or SYM Tools, you can log into your account, click the "gear" icon, click "Account", and in the Invoice History section you will find a list of all receipts that we have sent. The entire account's billing history is available in this section and contains a list of all the invoices we’ve sent.

How do I cancel my account? If you know the product you’re signed up for you can cancel your account at any time. Just log into your account, click the "gear" icon, click Account, scroll down, and click the Close my Account button. Please note that once you’ve cancelled you won’t be charged again, but you are responsible for all previously incurred charges.

Any further questions? You can submit a billing support inquiry and we’ll get back to you as quickly as we can.
